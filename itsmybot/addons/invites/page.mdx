# Invite Traker Plugin
This plugin allows you to track invites of users.

## Commands
<Properties>
  <Property name="/invites [user]">
    Shows the invites of the user.
  </Property>
  <Property name="/inviter <user>">
    Shows the inviter of the user.
  </Property>
  <Property name="/invite add-bonus <user> <amount>">
    Adds bonus invites to the user.
  </Property>
  <Property name="/invite remove-bonus <user> <amount>">
    Removes bonus invites from the user.
  </Property>
  <Property name="/invite set-bonus <user> <amount>">
    Sets the bonus invites of the user.
  </Property>
  <Property name="/leaderboard invites">
    Shows the invites leaderboard.
  </Property>
</Properties>

## Placeholders
<Properties>
  <Property name="%invitetracker_inviter%">
    The inviter of the user.
  </Property>
  <Property name="%invitetracker_invites%">
    The amount of invites the user has.
  </Property>
  <Property name="%invitetracker_regular%">
    The amount of regular invites the user has.
  </Property>
  <Property name="%invitetracker_bonus%">
    The amount of bonus invites the user has.
  </Property>
  <Property name="%invitetracker_left%">
    The amount of left invites the user has.
  </Property>
  <Property name="%invitetracker_fake%">
    The amount of fake invites the user has.
  </Property>
</Properties>
